Hi, I'm Ashley. I'm 22 years old and let me tell you a little about myself.

I decided to start my own website because just making one seems fun to make. I would’ve made one sooner, but I just never had time to make one, and this time I have to make one for a class I am currently taking. That says a lot about me already, I tend to sometimes procrastinate but who doesn’t right? It’s a habit to always try to be the best person you can but sometimes people mess up and I think that’s okay (to some extent). I’m currently attending the University and perusing a degree in Information Systems. One of my favorite hobbies is practicing my photography skills. I’m still an armature photographer but I’ve always had an interest of it since high school. When I was attending high school, I would take my camera to school and take photos of my friends and upload them on Facebook. The reason I never persuaded photography as a career was because I just didn’t want it to become something stressful, as well something I would eventually dislike. This comes from a stigma that a job is a job, you could enjoy it, but it’ll eventually become stressful. I’m getting back into photography, it has been difficult journey because of the burn out I have been feeling with going to school and working. This year is going to change. My favorite things to photograph are people, especially my friends. This is what my blog it’s a little piece of the internet where I am able to express myself and my thoughts.
